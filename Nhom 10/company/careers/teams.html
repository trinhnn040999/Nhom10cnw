Engineering

Our engineers like to get their hands dirty and build stuff with every effort put toward unleashing the potential of teams that run on Atlassian software.

Whether crafting elegant JavaScript, building mobile apps, or architecting a scalable cloud platform with micro-services on AWS, our engineering team creates solutions that enable teams all across the world to dream, build, and ship what’s next.

Browse Engineering roles

Product Management

Our Product Managers are passionate about customer needs and understanding how to translate those into products and features used by millions daily.

We aim to excel at the key pillars that define our approach to product management: Excellent communication, the ability to lead & inspire, driving tangible outcomes, and always striving to master the craft.

Browse Product Management roles

Analytics & Data Science

The Analytics & Data Science team uses the power of data and analytics to drive better product decisions, smarter products, and drive operational success to scale Atlassian. With various backgrounds ranging from computer science to economics, our team helps our customers across the world achieve more.

Browse Analytics & Data Science roles

Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)

Our Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) is a rapidly growing team focused on building our tools, teams, and systems in a concerted effort to accelerate the cross-cutting reliability focal areas that aid Cloud Engineering as they grow and scale. SRE works alongside both our product family and platform developers to maintain and improve services and performance.

Browse SRE roles

IT

IT touches every aspect of Atlassian's business, while primarily focusing on providing value to both our customers and teams internally. We're a team that relies on people and technology in equal measure.

Responsible firstly for running a technology-first workplace, IT also manages our e-commerce & support platform, drives digital transformation, manages business systems, and intelligent automation.

Browse IT roles

Security

Security Engineering is integral to everything we do here at Atlassian. Our goal is to successfully protect and defend Atlassian’s infrastructure, products and networks from both anticipated and unforeseen cybersecurity threats.

Within our team you’ll find a number of specialists across Product Security, Ecosystem Security, Security Intelligence, Corporate Security, Security Development, and Trust Security.

Browse Security roles

Customer Experience

Our Customer Experience team is a collection of Product, Content and Service Designers, Researchers, Accessibility Experts, Program Managers, and Coaches using elegant design to help millions of customers achieve more.

We partner closely with product and engineering to shape the vision, develop the roadmap, and ship experiences that create value for all.

Browse Customer Experience roles

Be part of this remarkable journey helping customers across the world achieve more

People

Atlassian is continuing to grow at lightning speed, and the People team is at the center of that expanse. We’re a nimble group of motivated, creative professionals who view ourselves as ambassadors of Atlassian’s culture, attracting and hiring people who can elevate everything we do across the company.

Browse People roles

Finance & Accounting

Our Finance & Accounting team does much more than crunching numbers or ensuring everyone gets paid on time. We’re a trusted group helping to power the daily operations through short- and long-term strategies. We are a global team with an expansive variety of backgrounds, experiences, and talents that enable us to continue pushing forward.

Browse Finance & Accounting roles

Support (CSS)

Atlassian's Customer Support and Success team aims to create customers for life through honest conversations, transparency, and responsive problem-solving.

We look for problem solvers and stellar communicators with product insight — people who thrive in fast-paced environments and who are motivated by pushing the Atlassian experience forward.

Browse Support roles

Marketing

Creativity supported by data and outside disciplines can yield both unexpected and inspired results. Our Marketing team is a collection of writers, illustrators, thinkers — and, most importantly, communicators — who look to not only solve existing problems but look ahead for future ones.

Browse Marketing roles

Sales

Our goals go beyond wanting to sell a product. We harness the strength of our sales and operations teams to support our customers by reinventing how software gets sold so that we can operationalize and manage our GTM strategy to scale, grow, and continuously add value for our customers.

Browse Sales roles

Legal

Our Legal team keeps our business moving smoothly by educating and empowering our key stakeholders. We’re a balanced and open organization that spans Commercial, Corporate, Risk, IP, Privacy, and Product law. We work side-by-side with our product teams to build products that continue to allow our customers to achieve more.

Browse Legal roles

Program Management

Atlassian's Program Managers support a variety of different business functions, both technical and non-technical, including Customer Experience, IT, Customer Support and Success, and People teams. We strive to be experts in four key pillars: driving outcomes, communication and collaboration, leading and inspiring, and mastering the craft. 

Browse Program Management roles
