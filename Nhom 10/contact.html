The Trello Community is made up of other Trello users and experts, and is a great way to get an answer quickly. It's perfect for how-to's, use case questions, or advice!

Trello Support is a team of Trello-employed Support Specialists, ready to answer any and all questions you may have. This is the best option for account billing questions, security concerns, or questions about Business Class.

Send us a Message

If you're able to, please log in to your account first. That will add some additional information to your request, and will help us respond to it more quickly.

If you're able to answer any of the following questions in your bug report, it may help us resolve the issue more quickly:

  • When did you start observing this bug?
  • Do you see the same issue after refreshing the page?
  • Do other people you work with see the same issue?
  • Does the bug still happen in a private browsing window? (Instructions for Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Edge)
  • Does the bug still happen if you use a different browser?
  • Is it possible that the issue is being caused by a browser extension or customization?

If the bug is something that's easier to show than explain, attaching a screenshot to your report may also help. (Instructions for Mac and Windows)
